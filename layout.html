About Us
TechGem project aims to empower young girls in underprivileged and rural communities by bridging the gap and enabling them to embrace technology. Through training, mentorship, and access to technology resources, the project aims to foster their skills, interests, and participation in technology-related fields. The primary goal of this project is to bridge the digital divide and create a more equitable society. We strive to ensure that young girls, regardless of their background or socioeconomic status, have equal opportunities to learn and utilize technology. We aim to empower them as problem solvers and provide them with a broader perspective.
Mission
The mission of this project is to empower young girls in underprivileged and rural communities by providing them with the necessary tools, resources, and support to embrace technology. Through training, mentorship, and access to technology resources, the project seeks to break down barriers and enable these girls to develop essential digital skills, fostering their confidence and preparing them for success in the digital age.
Vision
The vision of this project is to create a future where young girls in underprivileged and rural communities have equal opportunities to embrace and excel in technology. It envisions a world where every girl, regardless of her background, can access and leverage technology to unlock her potential, contribute to her community, and thrive in the rapidly evolving digital landscape.
